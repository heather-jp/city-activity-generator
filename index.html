Paris Activities
1 Day in Paris:
Marvel at the Eiffel Tower and soak up the panoramic city views. Take a stroll along the Seine River and admire the iconic landmarks lining its banks. Visit the Louvre Museum and see masterpieces like the Mona Lisa.

Weekend Break in Paris (2-3 Days):
In addition to the above, climb the Arc de Triomphe for breathtaking city views. Explore the charming neighborhood of Montmartre and visit the Sacré-Coeur basilica. Wander through the Jardin du Luxembourg and enjoy a picnic lunch.

Up to a Week in Paris:
Take a day trip to Palace of Versailles, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Immerse yourself in the world of art at the Musée d'Orsay or the Orangerie Museum. Discover hidden gems like the Marais district with its trendy shops and historic architecture. Sample delicious French cuisine in a cozy Parisian café or a Michelin-starred restaurant. Catch a cabaret show at the Moulin Rouge for an unforgettable Parisian experience.
